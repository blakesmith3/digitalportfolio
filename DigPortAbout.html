About Me

I was originally born in Sheffield, England into a family of 6 consisting of 1 brother, 2 parents and 3 sisters. I went to primary at Wharncliffe Primary School, Sheffield before moving to Wimblington at the age of 7 transferring to Westwood Primary School, March. I later moved to Wisbech at the age of 8 and continued education at Westwood before going to secondary at Neale-Wade Academy, March. I stayed at Neale-Wade throughout all of secondary before enrolling into College of West Anglia, Wisbech Campus. I initially started studying Level 1 Mechanics but soon transferred to Level 2 IT and I am now studying coding languages such as HTML, CSS and Javascript.
